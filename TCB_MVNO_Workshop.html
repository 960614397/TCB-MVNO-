A unique value proposition for TCB

Position TCB not as a telecom provider — but as the TCB Digital Financial Access Platform. The MVNO layer drives actual banking behaviour and extends reach into underserved markets.

"Your Bank. Your Connection. Your Everyday Advantage" This is not a telecom product. It's a financial inclusion and transaction engine embedded into everyday connectivity.
Banking SIM — Financial Inclusion Layer
A branded TCB SIM/eSIM enabling always-on access to banking services for underbanked and rural customers.
  • Zero-rated access to mobile banking (USSD + app)
  • Airtime/data rewards for account activity
  • Simplified mobile onboarding
✓ Banking access embedded into the mobile layer
Transaction Incentive Engine
Drive transactional behaviour through connectivity rewards — banks process transactions, TCB MVNO actively drives them.
  • Data/airtime rewards for deposits and transfers
  • Incentives for digital payment vs cash
  • Rewards for savings consistency
✓ Increased volumes + reduced cash reliance
SME & Merchant Connectivity Platform
Connectivity solutions for SMEs and informal traders banking with TCB.
  • Business SIM plans + POS connectivity
  • Real-time transaction alerts
  • Business usage analytics
✓ TCB becomes a business growth enabler
Agent Banking Enablement Layer
Dedicated connectivity platform for TCB's agent banking network.
  • Reliable SIM connectivity for agents
  • Real-time transaction processing
  • Liquidity and float monitoring
✓ Improved agent performance + reduced downtime
Salary & Employee Banking Integration
Connectivity bundled into salary accounts and employee banking — turning payroll into loyalty.
  • Monthly data/airtime linked to salary deposits
  • Incentives for using TCB as primary bank
  • Employee financial wellness tools
✓ Increased salary account stickiness
Credit & Lending Behaviour Platform
